Our services

Building & Construction

Our company provides building and construction services and offers a range of services related to the construction, renovation, and maintenance of structures. These services include designing and planning a project, obtaining permits and approvals, managing construction timelines and budgets, and providing ongoing maintenance and repair services.

Architectural & Structural Drawing

We provide structural and architectural drawing services and offer comprehensive design solutions for buildings and structures. State-of-the-art software and tools are used to create detailed drawings and plans that are essential for construction, renovation, and maintenance projects.

Land Survey & Sale of Land

At Merriment Consult, we have licensed surveyors who use advanced equipment and technology to produce accurate and detailed maps and reports. They should be knowledgeable about local and state regulations and be able to provide guidance on any zoning or land-use restrictions that may impact the sale of the property.

General Electricals

We provide a wide range of electrical services to meet the needs of both residential and commercial customers. Our highly trained and experienced electricians can handle any job, no matter how big or small. We offer installation, maintenance, and repair services for lighting fixtures, electrical panels, outlets, switches, and more.

Painting

Our company offers a comprehensive range of painting services to cater to the needs of both residential and commercial clients. Our experienced and skilled painters are well-equipped to handle any painting project, big or small, and are committed to delivering top-quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

Tiling

At Merriment Consult, we offer a wide range of tiling services to suit the needs of both residential and commercial clients. Our experienced team of skilled craftsmen is dedicated to providing high-quality workmanship and exceptional customer service.

Facility Management

Welcome to our facility management services! Our company offers comprehensive solutions to ensure that your facility is well-maintained, efficient, and safe. Our team of experts has the experience and knowledge to provide customized solutions to meet your specific needs. Contact us today to learn more about our facility management services.

Building Materials

Welcome to our building materials services! Our company provides a wide range of quality building materials to help you complete your construction projects. Site Supervision

We provide on-site management services to oversee all aspects of your construction project, including scheduling, budgeting, quality control, and safety management. Our experienced team will ensure that your project runs smoothly and efficiently.

Profile Setting

At our company, we are committed to helping you create a profile that reflects your unique talents and capabilities. Contact us today to learn more about our profile setting services and how we can help you stand out in today's competitive job market.

Traffic Studies

As a construction company, we understand the importance of traffic management and ensuring the safety of both motorists and construction workers on and around construction sites. Renovation

Our company provides a wide range of renovation services to transform your home or commercial property into the space you've always dreamed of. Our experienced team of designers, architects, and contractors work together to ensure that your renovation project is a success from start to finish.
